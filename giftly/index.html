About

Giftly is designed to be a simple and clean giveaway bot. Instead of adding hundreds of confusing features, the goal is to keep everything lightweight, fast and easy to use.


The bot is currently in an early testing stage, meaning features are still being improved and expanded.

Features

🎉 Button Entry Users join giveaways with a single click.
⚡ Lightweight Fast and simple without unnecessary features.
🔄 Reroll Winners Select new winners anytime.
⏹ End Giveaways Early Stop giveaways instantly with a command.
📋 Giveaway List View all active giveaways on the server.
🧪 Early Development More improvements and features coming soon.

Why Giftly?

Many giveaway bots become overly complicated with too many settings and commands. Giftly focuses on doing one thing well: running giveaways simply and cleanly.


If you want a minimal, easy-to-use giveaway bot, Giftly might be exactly what your server needs.
